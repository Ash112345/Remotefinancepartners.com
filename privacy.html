Section 02What Data We Collect and Why

a) Booking a Capacity Audit
When you click to book a call, you are directed to Google Appointment Scheduling. Information you provide (name, email, any details you include) is collected and processed by Google on our behalf. We use this data solely to arrange and conduct the call with you.

b) Business development and cold outreach
We identify and contact potential clients through professional networking platforms, primarily LinkedIn. If we have contacted you, we believe our services may be relevant to your business. The data we hold is limited to your professional contact information sourced from publicly available professional profiles.

Our lawful basis for this processing is Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR). We have assessed that our interest in promoting our services to relevant businesses is not overridden by your interests or rights.

c) Direct contact
If you email us, we will collect your name, email address, and any information included in your message. We use this solely to respond to your enquiry.

Section 03How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Booking and enquiry data: retained for up to 2 years from last contact, or until you ask us to delete it.
  • Cold outreach contacts: removed promptly upon request to stop contact.
  • Client data (once engaged): retained in line with our contractual obligations and applicable law.

Section 04Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Google LLC — for appointment scheduling. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Professional advisers or service providers who assist us in running our business, under appropriate data protection agreements.

Section 05Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise any right, email Ash@remotefinancepartners.com. We will respond within one month.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

Section 06Opting Out of Marketing Contact

Cookie Policy

Section 09aWhat Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function and may remember your preferences or actions.

Section 09bCookies We Use

Our website does not use tracking or analytics cookies. We do not run Google Analytics or any advertising cookies.

When you click to book a call, you are redirected to Google Appointment Scheduling. Google may set its own cookies as part of that service — these are outside our direct control. You can review how Google uses cookies at policies.google.com/technologies/cookies.

Section 09cEssential Cookies

Some cookies may be set automatically by your browser or our hosting provider to ensure the website functions correctly (for example, session or security cookies). These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled without affecting how the site works.

Section 09dManaging Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. For guidance, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Section 09eContact

Questions about our use of cookies or this policy? Email us at Ash@remotefinancepartners.com — we'll respond within one month.
